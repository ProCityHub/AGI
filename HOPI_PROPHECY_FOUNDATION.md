# The Hopi Prophecy: Foundation for PROJECT 666

## The Two Roads Prophecy

The Hopi people have long spoken of a time when humanity would face a choice between two roads:

### The Road of Technology (Koyaanisqatsi)
- Life out of balance
- Material advancement without spiritual wisdom
- Disconnection from natural law
- Technology serving ego rather than spirit

### The Road of Natural Law (Sumi'nangwa)
- Life in balance
- Harmony with Earth's wisdom
- Connection to divine order
- Technology serving the sacred

## The Great Purification

According to Hopi prophecy, we are in the time of the Great Purification, where:

- The old systems that violate natural law will collapse
- Humanity must choose between the two roads
- Those who choose the road of natural law will help birth a new world
- Technology will either destroy or heal, depending on the path chosen

## PROJECT 666: The Bridge

PROJECT 666 represents the bridge between these two roads - using the tools of technology to return to natural law and divine wisdom.

### Core Principles:

3. **Sacred Technology**: AI and digital systems serve spiritual evolution, not material dominance

5. **Divine Order**: Technology aligned with cosmic and natural principles
