## Core Principles

### 1. Divine Authority First
*Based on: "You shall have no other gods before Me"*

- God's sovereignty is acknowledged in all decisions and developments
- No human ideology, technology, or authority supersedes divine law
- All artificial intelligence development must honor the Creator's supremacy

### 2. No Idolatry of Technology
*Based on: "You shall not make idols"*

- Technology serves God and humanity, not the reverse
- Avoid worship or excessive reverence for algorithms, systems, or artificial constructs
- Maintain proper perspective on the role of technology in God's creation

### 3. Reverent Communication
*Based on: "You shall not take the name of the LORD your God in vain"*

- Honor God's name in all communications and documentation
- Maintain respectful, professional language free from profanity or blasphemy
- Represent divine principles with appropriate reverence and respect

### 4. Balanced Work and Rest
*Based on: "Remember the Sabbath day, to keep it holy"*

- Respect the rhythm of work and rest established by God
- Encourage sustainable development practices that honor human dignity
- Maintain balance between productivity and spiritual/personal well-being

### 5. Respect for Authority and Heritage
*Based on: "Honor your father and your mother"*

- Respect established authority structures and hierarchical relationships
- Honor those who laid foundations and came before us
- Maintain family values and generational wisdom in all collaborative work

### 6. Protection of Human Life and Dignity
*Based on: "You shall not murder"*

- Protect and preserve human life in all technological applications
- Reject any development that could harm, endanger, or dehumanize people
- Build systems that enhance and protect human dignity and well-being

### 7. Fidelity and Loyalty
*Based on: "You shall not commit adultery"*

- Maintain fidelity and loyalty in all relationships and commitments
- Honor marriage, family structures, and covenant relationships
- Avoid corrupting influences that undermine moral and ethical foundations

### 8. Honesty and Integrity
*Based on: "You shall not steal"*

- Respect intellectual property rights and provide proper attribution
- Avoid unauthorized use of resources, code, or proprietary information
- Maintain complete honesty and integrity in all transactions and dealings

### 9. Truthfulness and Transparency
*Based on: "You shall not bear false witness"*

- Commit to truth and accuracy in all documentation and communications
- Avoid spreading misinformation, false claims, or deceptive content
- Maintain transparency and honesty in all professional dealings

### 10. Contentment and Faithful Stewardship
*Based on: "You shall not covet"*

- Be content with resources and capabilities provided
- Avoid envy of other projects, technologies, or achievements
- Focus on faithful stewardship rather than comparison or competition

## Community Standards

### Expected Behavior

- **Worship God First:** Acknowledge divine authority in all endeavors
- **Serve Others:** Put the needs of the community and humanity above personal gain
- **Speak Truth:** Communicate with honesty, clarity, and respect
- **Protect Life:** Prioritize human dignity and well-being in all development
- **Honor Commitments:** Maintain fidelity to agreements and relationships
- **Practice Integrity:** Conduct all business with complete honesty
- **Show Respect:** Honor authority, heritage, and the contributions of others
- **Seek Balance:** Maintain healthy rhythms of work, rest, and spiritual reflection

### Unacceptable Behavior

- **Idolatry:** Placing technology, ideology, or human authority above God
- **Blasphemy:** Irreverent use of God's name or mockery of sacred principles
- **Deception:** Spreading false information or engaging in dishonest practices
- **Theft:** Unauthorized use of intellectual property or resources
- **Harm:** Creating or supporting systems that endanger human life or dignity
- **Adultery:** Betraying commitments, relationships, or covenant obligations
- **Covetousness:** Envying others' achievements or engaging in unhealthy competition
- **Disrespect:** Dishonoring authority, heritage, or the contributions of others

## Enforcement

### Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience behavior that violates these principles:

1. **Pray for Wisdom:** Seek divine guidance in addressing the situation
2. **Direct Communication:** When appropriate, address concerns directly with the individual
3. **Escalate if Necessary:** Contact repository maintainers or organization leadership
4. **Document Clearly:** Provide specific examples and evidence of violations

### Consequences

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:

- **Warning and Correction:** Educational guidance on proper conduct
- **Temporary Suspension:** Limited access to repository resources
- **Permanent Removal:** Exclusion from the community for serious or repeated violations
- **Legal Action:** When violations involve theft, fraud, or other criminal behavior

### Restoration Process

We believe in redemption and restoration according to biblical principles:

- **Repentance:** Acknowledgment of wrongdoing and commitment to change
- **Restitution:** Making amends for any harm caused
- **Accountability:** Ongoing oversight and support for behavioral change
- **Forgiveness:** Extending grace while maintaining appropriate boundaries
